How to Wear a Suit

When wearing a suit, make sure the jacket fits well. It should hug your shoulders and the sleeves should end at your wrist bone. The trousers should break slightly at the top of your shoes.

KEY POINTS

  • Proper shoulder fit is essential
  • Sleeves should show 1/2 inch of shirt cuff
  • Trousers should have a slight break at the shoes
