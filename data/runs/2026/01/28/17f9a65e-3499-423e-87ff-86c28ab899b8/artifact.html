Philosophy

The art of design is the art of organizing information.

The Method

We believe that design should be invisible. It should serve the content, not compete with it. Through the rigorous application of the grid, we achieve clarity, consistency, and objective truth.

Function

Design is determined by the function of the object. Ornamentation is removed to reveal the essence of the form.

Structure

Content is organized mathematically. The grid is the underlying structure that holds the design together.

Typography

Sans-serif typefaces are used for their neutrality and legibility. Hierarchy is established through size and weight, not decoration.

Asymmetry

Balance is achieved through unequal distribution of elements, creating dynamic tension and interest.
